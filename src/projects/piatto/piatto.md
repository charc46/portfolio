Piatto is a social dish discovery platform designed to enable users to find and enjoy new dishes based on their taste and that of their social network.

![Piatto](./cover.png)

## Technologies used:

1. Ruby on Rails
2. HTML
3. CSS/Sass
4. PostgreSQL
5. Hosted on Heroku
